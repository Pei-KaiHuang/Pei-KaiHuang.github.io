About me

I am currently a Phd Student at National Tsing Hua University. I've received the bachelor degree from Fujian Normal University in 2017, and the master degree from National Central University in 2019. I have reviewed papers from different CV/AI conferences, e.g., CVPR, ECCV, AAAI, ICME, BMVC, and ICIP, and biomedical related journals, e.g., TIFS, TIM, TBME, BSPC.

My research area focuses on face anti-spoofing and rPPG Estimation.
