About me

I am currently a Phd Student at National Tsing Hua University. I've received the bachelor degree from Fujian Normal University in 2017, and the master degree from National Central University in 2019. I have reviewed papers from different CV/AI conferences, e.g., CVPR, ECCV, AAAI, ICME, BMVC, and ICIP.

My research area is face anti-spoofing and rPPG.
